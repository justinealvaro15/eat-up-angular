About Us

Can't find what you're looking for?

LOOK NO MORE.

Eat UP is a food crowdsourcing progressive web application(PWA) and a go-to place for anyone who wants to find food, beverages, or food establishments, exclusively around the UP Diliman campus, that fit perfectly with their cravings or budget.

Currently, it covers 20+ food establishments found across the UP Diliman campus.
